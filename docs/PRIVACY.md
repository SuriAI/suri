# Privacy & Security

Suri is built to be a "Privacy-First". This means your data never leaves your computer, and you have full control over who gets scanned.

## 1. You Own Your Data
*   **Local Only**: Everything (names, logs, and face data) stays on your hardware. We don't have a "cloud" that sees your face.
*   **No Photos Stored**: Suri doesn't save photos of you. It turns your face into a string of numbers (a "face signature") and deletes the photo instantly.
*   **No Tracking**: We don't track how you use the app. No "phone home" signals, no analytics, no ads.

## 2. Consent is Required
We don't scan anyone "by accident." 
*   **The "Privacy Shield"**: If you haven't given permission (consent) for someone to be scanned, Suri will automatically **blur the camera feed** and hide their face behind a shield.
*   **Blocked Capture**: The "Capture" button is physically locked until you (the administrator) toggle the consent switch for that person.
*   **Persistent Proof**: Consent is saved in the database so you always have a record of who agreed to be part of the system.

## 3. Strong Security (Made Simple)
*   **Locked to your PC**: Your data is encrypted with a "Digital Lock" that stays on your computer. If someone steals your database files but doesn't have your specific computer, they can't read the face data.
*   **Hard Delete**: When you delete a person from Suri, we don't just "hide" them. We permanently wipe their face signature from the disk.
*   **Vault Backups**: When you export data, you lock it with a password. If you lose that password, even we cannot get your data back.

## 4. Compliance
Suri follows the rules of the **Philippine Data Privacy Act (DPA)** and **GDPR** by design. We focus on:
*   **Transparency**: Letting you know exactly what is being collected.
*   **Choice**: Giving people the right to say "No" to biometric scanning.
*   **Security**: Keeping data safe from unauthorized access.

## 5. Optional Cloud Features (SaaS)
If you choose to use our optional Cloud service for multi-branch sync or remote reporting:
*   **Encrypted Tunnel**: Your attendance records are sent through an encrypted tunnel.
*   **Shielded Biometrics**: Even in the cloud, your face signatures stay "scrambled" (E2EE). We do not have the keys to unlock them on our servers.
*   **Separation of Data**: The cloud sees that "Someone" checked in at "Branch A," but it doesn't "see" their face or identity unless you explicitly authorize it.
